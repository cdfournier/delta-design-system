# Banner

Banners are high-emphasis organism patterns that combine image surfaces and the Content molecule for hero and promotional layouts.

## Anatomy

- Container: rounded clipping surface for image and content composition
- Image layer: full background image or split figure panel
- Overlay (optional): dark tint on background-image variant (`overlay/dark`)
- Content layer: nested Content molecule (`title+p` or `h+p`)

## Usage

### Do

- Use `background-image` for hero-style moments and high-contrast imagery.
- Use `split` for editorial layouts where image and copy need equal weight.
- Reuse Content controls for eyebrow, CTA modes, and footer behavior.

### Don't

- Do not disable overlay on low-contrast photos if body copy remains white.
- Do not mix `title+p` and `h+p` in one banner instance.
- Do not hardcode spacing outside tokenized values.
