# Cards

Cards are flexible content containers that group related media and copy into scannable units.

## Anatomy

- Container: border, radius, and clipping surface
- Figure: optional media region at top or side
- Content: heading and body copy area

## Accessibility

- Use semantic `<article>` cards with meaningful heading hierarchy.
- Keep image `alt` text contextual to the card content intent.
- Preserve text contrast in both light and dark modes.

## Usage

### Do

- Use vertical cards for grids and galleries.
- Use horizontal cards for feature rows and list highlights.
- Keep body copy concise and scannable.

### Don't

- Do not mix multiple heading levels inside one card body without structure.
- Do not let media aspect ratios distort across variants.
- Do not remove borders when cards sit on mixed page surfaces.
