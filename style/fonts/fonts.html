Fonts

In both traditional typesetting and modern usage, the word “font” refers to the delivery mechanism of the typeface design. In traditional typesetting, the font would be made from metal or wood. Today, a font is a digital file.

Font Family

The Delta Design System uses Ovo as our headline font family and Montserrat as our body copy and all-purpose font family. The font weights we use for each depend on the use case, but are intended to provide the most legible option for each situation. We use the following Sass declarations in our style sheets to set the font-family:

  • $font-head: ("Ovo", serif);
  • $font-body: ("Montserrat", sans-serif);
  • $font-body-norm: 400;
  • $font-body-bold: 700;
